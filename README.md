# Favorite Movie: Inception
![Inception Poster](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/I/91ebheKQNbL._AC_UF894,1000_QL80_.jpg)

**Inception** is a *science fiction* film directed by **Christopher Nolan**, released in **2010**.

> *"An idea is like a virus. Resilient. Highly contagious. And even the smallest seed of an idea can grow."*  
> – Dom Cobb

## Why I Like It

- The plot is mind-bending and original — dreams within dreams!
- The direction and cinematography are outstanding.
- It has thought-provoking dialogue.
- The visual effects are stunning.
- Leonardo DiCaprio gives a powerful performance.

### Summary

Dom Cobb is a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from people’s dreams.  
But to earn his redemption, he must do the impossible: *inception* — planting an idea in someone’s mind.

---

*Inception* is more than just a movie — it's a journey into the mind.
